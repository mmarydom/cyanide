My name is Cole Pergerson

I'm currently a student studying digital media art at San Jose State Univeristy. My main focus is game development and I have been using Unity3d for about two years. Right now I'm interested in creating first person viewed games because those games force players to pay attention to their environment. This gives me the opportunity to make very detailed virtual levels. More importantly, interacting with the game mechanics is more intimate.

I like to take a different approach to exploring in games. Instead of roaming vast terrians, I prefer the player to examine the small details. Using your brain makes finding secrets more awarding. In my first person viewed games, I try to limit the amount of UI and instead give clues from the environment.

Current Project

Horizon Dream

An FPS game that explores our fears and ambitions. As a resistence commando, you'll climb up the Tower of Doom through traps, puzzles, evil spirits, and other challenges.
