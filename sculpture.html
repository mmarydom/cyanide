These are some of the 3D sculptures that I worked on from my Art 13: 3D Concepts class. I love digital media art but I am also a hands-on artist so I love getting my hands dirty inorder to free my creative mind.



I'm (not)Okay



Medium: Mixed Media, Plaster

Dimensions: 1920 x 1080


This piece is made from plster and other found objects. It represents that mentally ill and its coping devices. I am a strong advocate when it comes to mental health and I try to portray these ideas sometimes through my art pieces. To me, art is my coping mechanism and my medicine.
