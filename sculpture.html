I'm (not)Okay



Medium: Mixed Media, Plaster


This piece was made from plaster and other found objects, and was made to represent mental illness. Although it has this friendly and cute deameaner, it has a dark and sad underlying message. This piece was hugely inpsired by a movement that I adore in Japan called menhera, where one would decore themselves in a cute fashion but with dark accessories. This is a message to those around them that they are aware of their problems and they are not ashamed by it. Some even see their mental illnesses as a strength thanks to this movement.




Black Music Box



Medium: Wood and metal


I made this piece for my Art 13 laser cutter assignment. This small intricate box was largely referenced from the Black Box from the video game Nier Automate, one of my favorite games. This box acts as both a display piece and a music box, with interchange bottoms. Above is the file made with Adobe Illustrator, along with the finished piece being played.
